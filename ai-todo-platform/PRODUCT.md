# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Productivity users managing personal or team todos, using AI to enhance task management. Context: at work desks, home offices, or on mobile during commutes. Job: organize tasks, prioritize, get AI suggestions for improvements.

## Product Purpose
An AI-powered todo platform that helps users manage tasks efficiently, providing smart suggestions, prioritization, and insights to boost productivity. Success: users complete more tasks on time, feel less overwhelmed.

## Brand Personality
Efficient, intelligent, intuitive. References: Linear (clean, focused), Notion (flexible, smart), Raycast (fast, powerful).

## Anti-references
Cluttered interfaces like old Trello, confusing AI like generic chatbots without structure, over-designed apps that prioritize looks over function.

## Design Principles
- Efficiency first: minimize clicks, maximize clarity.
- AI as assistant: subtle, helpful, not intrusive.
- User control: empower users with smart defaults but full customization.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA compliance. Support for reduced motion, high contrast options, keyboard navigation. Consider color blindness with sufficient contrast ratios.
